## 영단어 소리내어 읽기

Read the following words aloud. Repeat each word several times to get comfortable with its sound.

Apple  
*애플* — 사과

Banana  
*버내너* — 바나나

Car  
*카* — 자동차

Dog  
*독* — 개

Cat  
*캣* — 고양이

House  
*하우스* — 집

Book  
*북* — 책

Phone  
*폰* — 전화기

Practice reading these words clearly. Focus on pronouncing each syllable.
